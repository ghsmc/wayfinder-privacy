Privacy Policy for Wayfinder Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

Wayfinder is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension processes your data locally on your device and only sends information to AI services that you explicitly authorize.

Data Collection and Usage

What We Collect

  • Resume Data: Only when you upload it through the extension
  • Form Field Data: Only the specific fields being filled on job application pages
  • API Key: Your Groq API key (stored locally in Chrome)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Personal browsing history
  • ❌ Other website data
  • ❌ Personal information beyond what you provide
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Usage statistics

Data Processing

Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage
  • Your resume and API key never leave your computer
  • No data is sent to our servers

AI Processing

  • Resume and form data is sent to Groq AI (your chosen provider) for processing
  • This happens only when you click "Autofill Application"
  • You control this through your own Groq API key and account
  • We have no access to your Groq account or billing

Data Transmission

  • Data is only sent to Groq AI servers (not our servers)
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • No data is stored on external servers

Your Rights

Data Control

  • You can delete your stored data anytime through the extension
  • You control your own AI API usage and billing
  • You can revoke access by changing your API key

Data Deletion

  • Clear all data: Use the "Clear Resume" button in the extension
  • Remove extension: Uninstalling removes all local data
  • API data: Managed through your Groq account

Third-Party Services

Groq AI

  • We use Groq AI for form mapping intelligence
  • You provide your own API key
  • Groq's privacy policy applies to their processing
  • We don't have access to your Groq account

Security

  • All local data is encrypted by Chrome's storage system
  • No unencrypted data transmission
  • No data sharing with third parties

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • GDPR (for EU users)
  • CCPA (for California users)
  • Standard web privacy practices

Your privacy matters. Wayfinder is designed to be privacy-first, with you in control of your data.
